Summit Credit Union



Student Free Checking

  I picked the Student Free Checking account from Summit Credit Union because it is a basic account that is cost-effective. Some of its features are:

  • Free Visa debit card
  • No monthly maintenance fee
  • No minimum balance required
  • Online banking
  • Mobile deposit (app)
  • Free ATM finder
  • Unlimited non-Summit ATM free withdrawals
  • Overdraft protection

Cash Boomerang Feature

  With the Cash Boomerang feature from Summit, you get money back every November, based on how much you save and borrow.

Primary Savings Account

  I chose the Primary Savings Account because it is a simple account, but comes with benefits such as:

  • 0.1% APY
  • No monthly fees
  • No minimum balance charges
  • Direct deposit
  • Open other accounts for different savings
  • Take out money whenever
